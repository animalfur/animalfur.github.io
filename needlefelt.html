How to Needlefelt

Needle felting is simple to do but complicated to learn. First, wash the wool. If the wool is not washed, sticks and other
things might get into your creations. To wash the wool, submerge the wool in water and dish soap, and take the sticks out.
Repeat the process until the wool is not oily and there are no more sticks in it. To make a flat shape,
use a foam block. Then, move the wool into a rough approximation of the shape they want to make, and poke the
outline all over with a needle, until the outline is firm. Then, fold the excess into
the main shape, and poke the wool all over until the whole thing is firm. After that, a 2-D shape will be firm!
To make a 3-D shape, roll the wool into a ball. Poke it all over until it is slightly firm but still squishy.
Then, poke the ball where the ball needs to compress, until the shape is clear and defined. That is how to needlefelt.

Step 1: Wash the wool

  1. Washing wool - important!

  2. Sticks + dirt can be in if you dont wash

  3. To wash, put wool in water and dish soap

  4. Repeat until all sticks and oil is gone

  5. Leave the wool out to dry

Step 2: Get supplies

Supplies

  1. 4 things you need to needlefelt:

    2. a. Wool
    b. Felting block
    c. Felting needles
    d. Thimble
  2. Wool comes in different colors, for intersting creations

  3. Felting blocks protect your leg or the table from the needle

  4. Needles come in different gauges

  5. The higher the gauge, the longer the needle

  6. The longer the needle, the sharper and the bigger creations it can make

  7. Thimbles protect your thumb from being cut

Step 3: Felt the wool

  1. Two options

  2. Flat shape or 3D shape

  3. First, move the wool into an approximation of the shape you want, for a flat shape

  4. Poke the outline all over until it is firm

  5. Fold the rough edges into the main shape

  6. Poke the shape all over until you have your creation!

  7. For a 3D shape, roll the wool into the shape you want.

  8. Poke it until it is firm

  9. Poke the shape in the place you want to compress

  10. You now have your shape!
